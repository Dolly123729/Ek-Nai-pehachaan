Step-by-Step AI Learning Guide

🟢 How to Use QuillBot Properly

Step 1: Write your own paragraph first.

Step 2: Paste into QuillBot to improve clarity.

Step 3: Compare changes and understand why.

❌ Don’t copy full essays.
✅ Use it to improve your own writing.

🔵 How to Use Perplexity for Research

Step 1: Ask a clear research question.

Step 2: Check the sources provided.

Step 3: Take notes in your own words.

❌ Don’t submit AI answers directly.
✅ Use it to understand topics deeply.

🟣 How to Use Canva for Projects

Step 1: Plan your content first.

Step 2: Use AI templates to design.

Step 3: Customize with your own ideas.

❌ Don’t rely only on templates.
✅ Add your creativity.

🟠 How to Use Notion AI for Study

Step 1: Upload class notes.

Step 2: Ask AI to summarize.

Step 3: Create flashcards from summary.

❌ Don’t depend on AI memory.
✅ Use it to organize your learning.
